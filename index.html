Two-Artist Practice

The Practice

Daniel & Tiffany Monteverde form a two-artist practice where engineering, architecture, craft, and light become a shared artistic language.

M.Eng.

Daniel Monteverde

Founder and Artistic Director of Monteverde Studio, Daniel transforms light into a universal language of harmony and connection.

With a Master's degree in Engineering and a lifelong sensitivity to form and texture, inspired early by his mother, a fashion designer, Daniel bridges the worlds of art, design, and technology.

For over two decades, he has served as Artistic Director for Seoul Luminarie, Taipei Luminarie, and Kobe Luminarie, shaping some of Asia's most celebrated light festivals.

Invited by the National Taiwan Museum of Fine Arts as the main artist for Writing of Light — 2020 Taiwan International Light Festival, he continues to create works that act as vessels of cross-cultural friendship and beauty, merging engineering precision with poetic expression.

B.Arch., M.Arch.

Tiffany Monteverde

Architect and Co-Director of Monteverde Studio, Tiffany brings a contemporary and global sensibility to the studio's creations.

Holding a Bachelor of Architecture from Cardiff University and a Master of Architecture from KU Leuven, she draws inspiration from the intricate geometries of Islamic art, its patterns, lattices, and enduring dialogue between light and structure.

Her approach reinterprets traditional architectural illumination with emotional depth and modern clarity.

Through her international vision and refined aesthetic, she contributes a fresh generational voice that infuses Monteverde Studio with balance, innovation, and grace.
